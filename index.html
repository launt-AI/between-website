01 · About Between

关 于 BETWEEN

a curated store for intimate life.

WHO WE ARE

BETWEEN 是一家"为亲密生活而挑"的集合买手店。

我们从世界各地挑选最有设计感、最被认真对待的产品——从私处护理到情趣，从避孕到香氛，让"亲密"这件事，回到它本该有的温柔与日常。

BETWEEN is a curated store for intimate life. We thoughtfully source the most beautifully designed pieces from around the world — from intimate care to playful pleasure, from protection to scent — bringing intimacy back to the tender, everyday space where it belongs.

THE NAME · 名字

BETWEEN——之间。

是两个人之间的距离，也是那一层薄薄的、温柔的"在场"。不是隔阂，是体贴；不是工具，是仪式。

BETWEEN — the space between two people. A thin, tender presence. Not a barrier — a kindness. Not a tool — a small ritual.

02 · Brand Story

品 牌 故 事

we don't talk about sex. we talk about being together.

我们想做一件很小、却很久没人认真做的事——把"亲密生活"这件事，从尴尬、羞耻、被回避的角落，搬回到日常的温柔里。

We wanted to do something small — yet something no one has really done with care: to take intimate life out of the awkward, hidden corner, and bring it back into the tenderness of every day.

在 BETWEEN，我们不谈"性"，我们谈"在一起"。
我们不卖"工具"，我们卖"温柔"。

从美国带回植物性的私处护理，从日本挑选最薄的避孕产品，从西班牙带回设计师手作的情趣小物，从世界各地，为"亲密生活"挑出值得被认真对待的每一件东西。

At BETWEEN, we don't talk about sex. We talk about being together. We don't sell tools. We sell tenderness.
